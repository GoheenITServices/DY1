Come and meet our school mascot!

Baron is an award winning Labrador Retriever.  Some of the kids call home him "the Bear!"  He is mild mannered, soft and handsome.  He comes to the center once or twice a week to spread love and joy to the kids.  As you can imagine, Baron is very popular at Discovery Years!
