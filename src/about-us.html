So What Makes Discovery Years a Great Choice?

There are many choices for childcare.  We think we are a better choice because we offer a home-style environment that includes fun, food and excellent learning with the feeling of being at home away from home.  We believe in cutting cookies, not in cookie-cutter childcare.

One of first things we do is teach your little one how to be friends, and we help them develop good relational skills and manners.  We enhance learning by using the amazing FrogStreet curriculum.  Kids get served hot meals from Miss Annie's kitchen each day!  And on some days they even get a visit from Discovery Years mascot:  Baron, the award winning Labrador Retriever...also known as "the Bear!"
